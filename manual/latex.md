# How to get started with Latex

## Basic Syntax

- $ $ (Math_mode)
- $$ $$ (Math_mode but more display space)
- $ \left( \right) $ (makes brackets bigger)

## Tidy up your writing

`` latex

\beg{<environment}  
 \item Hello
\item World
\end{<environment>}
``

- will display numbers and format what you have written
